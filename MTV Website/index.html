What is the MTV Challenge?

The Man to Void Challenge was originally created by Ayman to give the community extra content on the Global side of DFFOO

Now manned by TreeVoid, he issues a month long challenge where players must complete every Shinryu event with special restrictions

Restrictions such as:
Banned Synergy
Any unit that has synergy or is featured on the banners associated with the stage is banned.
Lockouts
You may only use a unit once during the whole month long challenge
Limited Bans
Hand selected power houses are banned for the month. Some months you may select a number of these units to unban
