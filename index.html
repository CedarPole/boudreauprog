Hello World! Welcome to Daniel Boudreau's personal website!

Born and raised in Canada, I always had a passion for technology, whether it was video games, or code.

My passion for programming started with a programming class in high school, where I learned action-script. To continue this passion, I attended a private college, where I finished my degree of Advanced Web Development with honors. I learned such programming languages as XHTML, CSS, Java, ASP.net, SQL Server, JQuery, as well as how to use Photoshop. Unfortunatly , my OJT fell through, and with little to no work around my small town, I concentrated on his current job.

Let's fast foreward a couple years, from 2011 to 2017, where I met my now husband. Moving to Texas, USA to be with him, I took that oppertunity for a fresh start in a new city to revisit his passion. With my free time, I am interning for a Podcast, where I creates and designs streaming templates. I am also relearning all of the programming languages from my college years, starting from the ground up. So far I've relearned HTML5, CSS, ASP.net, and Java. I'm currently looking for a new career in the IT field, hoping to finally make my passion into a career,

So far I hope you enjoyed my little blog, and feel free to check out my completed project in my portfolio, as well as to contact me via any of my social media!
